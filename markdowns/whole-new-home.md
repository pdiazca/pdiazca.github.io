# Background

Prior to becoming Kabuni, Whole New Home was a platform that connected interior decorators and designer professionals directly with homeowners. It had over 2340 registered Home Designers and a portfolio of over 40,000 home décor and furniture products.


## My Role
I was hired by Whole New Home to help make the switch from a PHP based web application to a full stack Ruby on Rails application.

I was in charge of the front end development of the switch, as well as being on the UI/UX team to drive the app forward.
